01 — What it is

What is ContentIQ

ContentIQ is a content intelligence tool, built to track how your content actually performs over time, not a single snapshot, but the full trend. It pulls post level metrics into one place, keeps history that the native dashboard doesn't, and turns raw numbers into a clear read on what's working.

02 — Why this exists

Insight, not a subscription tier

Your LinkedIn metrics are a form of professional wealth, quietly compounding or quietly slipping away, and understanding them is part of how anyone navigates an increasingly competitive professional world. Most tools that offer this kind of insight put it behind a paywall or a usage tier.

It's the same pattern I wrote about in The Rise of Agentic Software Development Platforms, free tiers quietly shrinking as compute costs rise, real capability locked a subscription level higher, the tools with the most useful insight reserved for whoever pays the most.

ContentIQ is built the opposite way. No paywall, no subscription tier gating the real feature. The insight belongs to the person who made the content, not a vendor renting it back to them.

03 — What it does

Core capabilities

  • Post level tracking

    Impressions, engagements, and reach, held over time instead of overwritten week to week.

  • Pillar and format breakdown

    See which content pillar and format, original, repost, repost with commentary, actually earns engagement.

  • Per-post growth tracking

    Every post gets its own page showing exactly how it grew from the day it was published.

  • Weekly trend rollups

    Account-wide impressions and engagement, always current, no manual recalculating.
