01 — What it is

What is ContentIQ

ContentIQ is an internal content intelligence tool, built to track how LinkedIn content actually performs over time, not a single snapshot, but the full trend. It pulls post level metrics into one place, keeps history that the native dashboard doesn't, and turns raw numbers into a clear read on what's working.

02 — Why this app

Why build it

01

Native analytics are shallow and short lived. Once a post ages out of the window, the data is gone for good.

02

Third party tools solve this, but charge monthly for something straightforward enough to own outright.

03

Building it is itself a real, hands on data and AI credential, not just a tracker.

03 — What it does

Core capabilities

  • Post level tracking

    Impressions, reactions, comments, and shares, held over time instead of overwritten week to week.

  • Pillar and format breakdown

    See which content pillar and format, original, repost, repost with commentary, actually earns engagement.

  • Trend timeline

    A running view of impressions and engagement across weeks and months, not a single day's export.

  • Weekly KPI summary

    7 and 30 day rollups, always current, no manual recalculating.
